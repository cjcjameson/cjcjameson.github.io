Hi there! I'm C.J. Jameson. I'm an Engineering Manager at Monte Carlo Data.
What's my work style? My resume lists ENTP, Adaptability, Connectedness, etc. But here’s what they mean.
I think on my feet, pattern-match, and generate abstractions. I talk through discoveries and use metaphors to grapple with new code blocks. I’m an intuitive learner, so “aha moments” push me to the next level of understanding an engineering problem.
I adapt and think ahead because all too often, "the business needs have changed" is the norm. I anticipate my code's connections with other modules, and think ahead about how to test my code.
I listen carefully to my colleagues and contribute heavily to team discussion. I'm "plugged in" to the teams around me and I rejoice in helping other devs past a barrier that I have surmounted. Above all, I am empathetic to each person around me. "Go-giver"
